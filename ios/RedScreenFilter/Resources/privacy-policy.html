Privacy Policy for Red Screen Filter

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Summary in Plain English

Red Screen Filter is a privacy-focused app for Android and iOS. We do not collect, sell, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Settings and usage statistics stay on your device. Optional features (such as location for sunset scheduling or notifications for reminders) only run if you enable them.

Introduction

Red Screen Filter explains how the mobile application handles information on Android and iOS.

Information Stored on Your Device

We store the following only on your device. We do not upload this data to our servers:

  • Overlay and display preferences (opacity, color variant, presets)
  • Scheduling settings (times, sunset/sunrise options)
  • Automation and wellness preferences
  • Usage analytics (local only): overlay events, duration, streaks—shown only to you in the app
  • Location (optional): used only to calculate local sunset and sunrise when enabled; not sent to us

What We Do Not Do

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data for advertising or third-party analytics.

The app does not use Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, ad networks, user accounts, or remote tracking.

Platform-Specific Permissions

Android

  • Display over other apps: show the red overlay above other apps.
  • Foreground service: keep the overlay active in the background.
  • Notifications (optional): status and wellness reminders.
  • Location (optional): sunset/sunrise scheduling on device only.
  • Usage access (optional): app exemption feature; not stored permanently.

Google Play location APIs apply only if you enable location scheduling (Google Privacy Policy). Android may back up preferences if device backup is enabled.

iOS

  • Location (When In Use) (optional): sunset/sunrise scheduling on device only.
  • Notifications (optional): schedule and eye-strain reminders.
  • Camera (optional): ambient light estimation when auto-brightness is enabled (ARKit). No photos or videos are recorded or uploaded.
  • Siri Shortcuts: processed by Apple if you use shortcuts (Apple Privacy Policy).

The in-app overlay is visible only while you use Red Screen Filter. System-wide filtering uses Apple Accessibility Color Filters that you enable yourself. Widget data stays in an on-device App Group only.

Data Security

  • Data stays on your device unless you use OS backup (iCloud or Google backup).
  • No cloud account is required.
  • Uninstalling the app removes its local data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

  • View and change settings in the app.
  • Uninstall to delete local data.
  • Revoke permissions in system settings at any time.

Changes

We may update this policy by changing the Last Updated date and showing an in-app notice for material changes.
